The Blue Leopard

A Classic Restaurant with a Modern Approach


The Blue Leopard is the place to come for a relaxed restaurant atmosphere with modern decor. Our drinks list that boast a large range of Malaysian exotic food and drinks. We've been informed that our attic is haunted so in honor of our supernatural guests, we have a Watch Party every Saturday night from 7pm until close. Live entertainment seven nights a week, and never a cover charge for entry.
